About Insulation

Insulating your house is a crucial step in making it more energy-efficient and comfortable to live in. With proper insulation, you can reduce your energy bills and keep your home warm in the winter and cool in the summer.

Types of Insulation

  • Batt insulation: Made of fiberglass or rock wool, batt insulation is a common type of insulation that comes in rolls or blankets.
  • Blown-in insulation: Made of cellulose or fiberglass, blown-in insulation is typically used to fill in gaps or areas that are hard to reach.
  • Spray foam insulation: Made of polyurethane, spray foam insulation is ideal for filling in small gaps and cracks and can create an air barrier.

Insulation Tips

  • Insulate your attic to prevent heat loss.
  • Seal any gaps or cracks around windows and doors to prevent drafts.
  • Consider adding insulation to your walls and floors to further improve energy efficiency.
  • Hire a professional if you're unsure about the insulation process.
